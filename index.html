Mnemeo - a smarter flashcard app

For years, I was a huge fan of Memrise. After they changed direction, I couldn't find a replacement I loved, so I built my own. It combines a clean and modern UI with flexible study modes like typing and rating. It runs on a balanced algorithm that avoids the harsh resets of traditional SRS; it respects your long-term progress instead of punishing a single mistake. With easy import from Anki and CSV, it's a more effective way to study.
